Trim Levels

  • Platinum
  • XL
  • King Ranch
  • Active
  • Tremor

Platinum

The Platinum trim represents the pinnacle of Expedition luxury, offering heated and ventilated leather front captain's chairs with memory settings and premium interior appointments. Drivers seeking ultimate refinement will appreciate the genuine wood accents, power-folding mirrors with auto-dimming, and the available High Output engine producing an impressive 440 horsepower.

XL

The XL trim provides an excellent entry point into Expedition ownership with essential features that deliver outstanding value. This trim includes cloth front captain's chairs, an 8-way power driver seat, and 18-inch dark alloy painted aluminum wheels paired with all-terrain tires.

King Ranch

The King Ranch trim celebrates Texas heritage with distinctive Del Rio leather seating and western-inspired design elements throughout the cabin. Drivers will enjoy the 22-inch diamond cut machined face aluminum wheels, adaptive suspension, and the premium B&O Unleashed Sound System with 22 speakers as standard equipment.

Active

The Active trim appeals to adventure-seeking families who want versatility without compromise, offering both rear-wheel and four-wheel drive configurations. This trim features cloth front captain's chairs with 10-way power adjustment, available ActiveX-trimmed seating, and the capability to seat up to eight passengers comfortably.

Tremor

The Tremor trim is engineered for those who crave off-road capability combined with everyday comfort, featuring the High Output 440-horsepower engine as standard equipment. Drivers in Mount Pleasant will appreciate the 3.73 electronic locking axle ratio, all-terrain tires, and distinctive Tremor-branded leather seating with accent stitching.
