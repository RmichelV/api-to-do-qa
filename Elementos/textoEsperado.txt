Kia Niro FAQs
Is the Kia Niro a hybrid?
Yes, the Kia Niro is a hybrid vehicle that uses a four-cylinder gas engine paired with a 32-kW electric motor. It combines to give you ample power and standout efficiency behind the wheel.

How much cargo space is available on the Kia Niro?
Despite its small size, the Niro Hybrid boasts a surprisingly spacious interior. It offers more rear headroom than the Honda CR‑V and greater rear legroom than the Toyota RAV4. Its cargo capacity is 22.8 cubic feet, larger than the Toyota Corolla Cross Hybrid but a bit less than some other non-hybrid small SUVs.

What is the starting price of the 2026 Kia Niro?
The 2026 Kia starts at $27,090 MSRP or the LX trim, with higher trims ranging up to $35,490 MSRP for the SX Touring (plus destination and fees).
